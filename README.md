Problem: Mobile retail businesses in India generate thousands of sales transactions but lack a unified view to make fast, confident decisions on inventory, city expansion, brand performance, and customer targeting.
Solution: An interactive Sales Intelligence Dashboard that transforms raw transactional data into a real-time decision-making tool — answering the questions that actually matter to a retail business manager.
Key business questions answered:
Which brand generates the most revenue vs. most units? (they're not always the same)
Which cities are underperforming relative to their potential?
How is revenue trending month-over-month and year-over-year?
What payment infrastructure should the business prioritize?
Which models to restock, and who is buying them?
